ScanLite QR Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

Overview

ScanLite QR is a lightweight QR and Barcode scanner designed to provide fast and secure scanning functionality.

Information Collection

ScanLite QR does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal information.

Camera Permission

The application uses the device camera solely for scanning QR codes and barcodes. All processing takes place locally on the user's device.

Scan History

Scan history is stored locally on the device only. The data is never uploaded to any server and is never shared with third parties. Users can delete individual history items or clear all history at any time.

Third-Party Services

The application does not use analytics, advertising networks, tracking tools, or third-party SDKs that collect user information.

Data Sharing

No user data is shared with any third party.

Children's Privacy

The application does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
